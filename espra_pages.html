I am an autodidact born in Paris, France in 1984. I currently live in Madrid, Spain. I am looking for an immediate aesthetic experience. I use all that the real world contains of mysterious and invisible even in what is more mundane or ugly.

My works is inspired by music and have photographic bases. Painting only comes last, bringing its organic appearance. It allows me to extract my creations from the stream of immediate images, to hurt the image.

I love Art. Beyond the messy concepts, the awkward attempts and the refreshing happiness that creativity provides, a question makes me continue: where does it lead?
